How to Play the Memory Game

Game Rules

  • The game starts with a grid of face-down cards.
  • Your goal is to find all matching pairs of cards.
  • Click on a card to flip it over and reveal its value.
  • Flip two cards at a time. If they match, they stay flipped.
  • If the cards don't match, they will flip back after a short time.
  • Match all pairs to complete the level!

Tips and Tricks

  • Take your time to memorize the card positions.
  • Try to find matches in a logical sequence to avoid confusion.
  • Focus on one area of the grid at a time.
